Guidelines

  • Upload grayscale or RGB cardiac MRI scans
  • Recommended resolution: 128×128 or higher
  • Maximum file size: 10 MB
  • Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG

What happens next?

  1. Image is preprocessed and normalized
  2. U-Net model generates segmentation mask
  3. 12-class classifier predicts condition
  4. Results display with confidence score

Medical Disclaimer

This tool is for research and educational purposes only. All predictions must be reviewed by a licensed cardiologist before any clinical decision.
