Process

How It Works

From MRI scan to AI-powered segmentation in four simple steps.

01

Upload MRI Scan

Upload cardiac MRI files through our secure web interface. Supports multiple formats and batch processing.

02

AI Preprocessing

Automated intensity normalization, noise reduction, and spatial alignment using OpenCV pipelines.

03

U-Net Segmentation

Our trained U-Net model processes the image and generates precise pixel-level segmentation masks.

04

Clinical Report

Review interactive overlays, volume metrics, and classification results with confidence scores.
