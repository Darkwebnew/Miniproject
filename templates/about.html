About HeartSeg AI

AI-powered cardiac MRI segmentation built as a research project at Saveetha Engineering College.

Project Mission

HeartSeg AI was developed to address the critical bottleneck in cardiac MRI analysis: manual segmentation is time-consuming, requires expert radiologists, and produces inconsistent results.

Our U-Net deep learning model automates the segmentation of Left Ventricle, Right Ventricle, and Myocardium — achieving 94.8% accuracy while reducing analysis time from hours to seconds.

  • Automated pixel-level cardiac MRI segmentation
  • 12-class disease classification pipeline
  • Web-based clinical decision support interface
  • Optimized for rapid inference on standard hardware
